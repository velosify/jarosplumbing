A Local, Family-Run Plumbing Company You Can Trust

Jaros Plumbing has been proudly serving the Carnegie area and greater Pittsburgh community since 2009. FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Carnegie, PA and proudly serve the surrounding Pittsburgh communities including Bridgeville, Mt. Lebanon, Scott Township, Heidelberg, Greentree, and the rest of the South Hills. Not sure if we cover your area? Just give us a call.

Do you handle both residential and commercial plumbing?

Yes. While we specialize in residential service plumbing, we also handle commercial properties of all sizes — from minor repairs to large-scale installations and renovations.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Josh is a Licensed Master Plumber in Allegheny County (HP4172) and a licensed home improvement contractor in the state of Pennsylvania. Jaros Plumbing is fully insured for your protection.

Do you offer emergency service?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency plumbing service for burst pipes, severe leaks, and other urgent issues that can't wait. Call (412) 440-3041 anytime.

How much will my repair cost?

Pricing depends on the scope of the work. We always provide a clear, written estimate before starting any job — no surprises and no hidden fees.

How quickly can you come out?

For most non-emergency calls we can typically schedule within 1–2 business days. Emergencies are prioritized and addressed as quickly as possible — often the same day.
